How to use our bug trecker

  1. To get started, you need to upload at least one .py file (click upload new file)

  2. The file you uploaded will immediately appear in the left menu.

    -- Pay attention, when you load an existing file, its contents are overwritten, and its version is increased by 1

  3. Now you have the opportunity to go to this file to conduct tests

  4. On the tests page, you will find information about the file, such as: name, version, tests performed and errors in them

  5. Also, inside one test, you can view information about various errors by clicking the corresponding button

  6. In the reports tab, you can download reports for those files where tests were carried out at least once
